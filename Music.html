What is Music?

Music is a combination of sounds. It can reach people on social, emotional, and intellectual levels. It's academic(higher thinking), physical(coordination), and emotional(art form).

Importance

Music is important. It can give people a way to express themselves. Music can also bring people together. It can even cure depression. When we listen to music, it benefits our brain. Music can help people relax, relieve anxiety, and relieve pain.

Music Genres

A music genre is a conventional category that identifies some pieces of music. A few popular genres are rock, R&B(Rhythm and Blues), pop, and country.
